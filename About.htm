About us - Our Joga Bonito

By Henry Wilson

Joga bonito - the beautiful game (Portuguese) Popularised by the great Brazil team most notably led by Pele in the 1960s, the beautiful game belongs to all of us - the fans. It is Our Joga Bonito.

Our Joga Bonito aims to provide interesting, thoughtful analysis about the beautiful game to help fans understand it further. If you want to help spread the message, DM us on Instagram @OurJogaBonito.com.
